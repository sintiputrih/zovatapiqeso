GoFundme

The fundraiser is being coordinated by Amy Meehan to support the Pope family during this difficult time. They experienced the heartbreaking loss of Annemarie on August 8, 2023. Additionally, the funds will help cover costs associated with sports activities. Annemarie’s deep love for her boys is evident, and the Pembroke community is expected to come together to support the family. Currently, a total of $16,675 USD has been raised, surpassing the initial target of $15,000.
