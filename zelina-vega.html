Zelina Vega - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Facts, Career, Married

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Table of Biography

Zelina Vega is an American wrestler, manager as well and actress. At present, she is signed to WWE. In WWE, Zelina Vega performs on the Raw brand under the ring name Zelina Vega. She is also famous for working in Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA) as Rosita, and many other independent promotions under her real name. Moreover, she was a one-time Knockouts Tag Team Champion with Sarita.

Early Life and Childhood

The real and full name of Zalina Vega is Thea Megan Trinidad. Zelina was born on December 27, 1990. Her birthplace is in Queens. Currently, she is 31 years old. Her zodiac sign is Capricorn. The nationality of wrestler is nationality and her ethnicity is Puerto Rican descent.

Also, Zelina Vega was born to Michael Trinidad and Monique Ferrer. She has a younger brother named Timothy. From an early age, she grew up watching professional wrestling with her father. She considers that her father let her enter the wrestling business. Unfortunately, her parents separated when she and her brother were still young. However, the ex-married couple remained friends.

During her school days, Vega used to practice swimming and baseball. Sadly, her father expired in the September 11 attacks when she was just 10. Her father was a telecom analyst for Cantor Fitzgerald.

Professional Life

At the time when her career was not started, Zelina Vega used to work as a counselor at a camp for children who had lost their parents on 9/11. Similarly, she also participated in charitable causes such as Habitat for Humanity with Tuesday’s Children after Hurricane Katrina.

Wrestling career

The 17-year-old girl began her training under Javi-Air, Azriel, and T.J. Perkins. Her professional wrestling debut was under the ring name Divina Fly for National Wrestling Superstars (NWS) on February 20, 2010. It was in Bloomfield, New Jersey.

In 2010, she made her debut for Women Superstars Uncensored (WSU). It was under the ring name Divina Fly in a losing effort against Brittney Savage.

In 2011, she wrestled in a tryout dark match at Total Nonstop Action Wresting’s (TNA) Impact! On February 10, she debuted as the storyline cousin of Sarita under the ring name Rosita. Afterward, Rosita and Sarita performed various matches together. As Rosita, she appeared on AAA’s television program. She promoted a storyline where wrestlers from TMA were invading the promotion.

On 2011, May 14, Rosita made her debut in-ring debut for Family Wrestling Entertainment (FWE) at the Meltdown pay-per-view. In 2015, she joined GFW (Global Force Wrestling) and debuted for the promotion in June. Later, her profile was removed from the GFW website in August of the same year.

From 2013 to 2016 she took part in a tryout for WWE. She also made several brief appearances in the promotion in 2014. In 2017, she signed a contract with WWE and also initiated training at the WWE Performance Center. In 2018, Vega made her debut for the brand on the May 15 episode of Smackdown.

Other appearances

Trinidad aka Zelina appeared in the 2012 movie names Dorothy and the Witches of Oz. Similarly, she appeared in the documentary Children of 9/11. This also includes the first anniversary of the September 11 Memorial Ceremony. She participated in the reading of victim names.

Likewise, she portrayed wrestler AJ Lee in the 2019 movie titled Fighting with My Family. She claims that she is inspired to act by Dwayne Johnson. Moreover, she has also made an appearance as a manager in WWE 2019 and a playable character in WWE 2020.

Awards and Nominations

Zelina Vega won a match against newcomer Candy Cartwright with a Fly Clutter on September 11, 2010. She was awarded ‘Inspirational Wrestler of the Year’ by the Magazine Pro Wrestling Illustrated’ in 2011.

Subsequently, she ranked No.31 of the top 50 female wrestlers in the ‘PWI Female 50′ in the same year. In 2018, American Sports Magazine Sports Illustrated’ ranked her at No. 29 in the ‘top 30 female wrestlers’ 2018. Surprisingly, Zelena and Sarita won the ‘TNA Knockouts Tag Team Championship’ together.
