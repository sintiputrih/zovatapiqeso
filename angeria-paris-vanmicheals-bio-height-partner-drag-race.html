Who Is Angeria Paris VanMicheals? Bio, Height, Partner, Out Of Drag

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, May 9, 2024

It’s already past 5 eps of RuPaul’s Drag Race season 14 since it premiered on January 7, 2022, and so far (8 Feb 2022), the strongest appeared to be Angeria Paris VanMicheals. With already 2 wins and the rest challenges safely ventured, she might just be the victor of this season.

This Angeria Paris VanMicheals Bio explores her life.

Angeria Paris VanMicheals On RuPaul’s Drag Race

Like always, RuPaul’s Drag Race season 14 began introducing its cast on its very first ep. But this season, the production decided to make it a two-part premiere with only 7 contestants introduced in the first half, and the rest 7 on the 3rd episode.

Joining Angeria this season were co-contestants — Alyssa Hunter, Bosco, Daya Betty, DeJa Skye, Jasmine Kennedie, Jorgeous, June Jambalaya, Kerri Colby, Kornbread “The Snack” Jeté, Lady Camden, Maddy Morphosis, Orion Story, and Willow Pill.

An old-school pageant queen, Angeria lit up the show with her charisma, uniqueness, nerve, and talent. No wonder, she was the victor of the 2nd and 4th eps. Sadly, Daya, Orion, Kornbread, Alyssa, and June have already taken their leave by ep 5.

Back on Jan 15, 2022, all excited to make her debut on VH1, Angeria took it to her FB with a pic of the cast, captioned, “On tonight, I am officially making my national television debut on VH1 at 8 pm for the 2nd premiere of Rupauls Drag Race Season 14 !! I am so grateful and beyond excited!!”

Angeria Paris VanMicheals Out Of Drag

Out of Drag, Angeria is just your average brown-eyed guy with a circular goatee, mustache, and short hair. Her style preference outside drag is mostly tees, trousers, and hats.

Also, she’s got a tattoo over her right shoulder.

Here’s a real pic of Angeria.

What Is Angeria Paris VanMicheals Real Name?

Angeria’s real name wasn’t revealed.

As for her drag name origin, she got it from a girl she went to college with. Her name was Angera, but to make it fancier, she added an “I” in front of the “A.”

Previously, she went by the name “Malaysia Payne.”

Angeria Paris VanMicheals Height

Angeria Paris VanMicheals stands tall at a height under 5 feet 11 inches (180 cm).

She stood on par with most of her co-contestants.

Angeria Paris VanMicheals Partner

Angeria Paris VanMicheals was “single” according to her FB.

However, we’ve seen her getting “very close” with Lady Camden on RuPaul’s Drag Race season 14. So close that, the pair discovered a mutual fascination with more than just their drag styles — including their taste in adult films, which led to “true love at first porn.”

In case you missed the ep, Angeria shared that she asked Lady Camden if she could borrow her porn, to which, Camden replied, “Yeah b*tch, you can!”

Besides Lady Camden, Angeria was also seen getting fascinated by Maddy Morphosis, the first straight, cisgender man to ever join the show. She even labeled the “most sweet, talented, and brave” Maddy to be her MCM on IG.

But Maddy was dating the drag queen Liza TheQueen (real name: Jennifer Standridge) at the time. They were together ever since March 2017.

So, it doesn’t seem like Angeria partnered with anyone on the show.

What Does Angeria Paris VanMicheals Do For A Living?

Angeria, a.k.a. “Angie” is a full-time drag who is the epitome of high glamour, and Atlanta drag pageantry at its absolute finest. This star was on her way to “conquer the world” with her “sweet spirit and unwavering love, and passion for drag.”

And it seems that Angeria might just conquer it! Besides her chances at winning  RuPaul’s Drag Race, Angeria was getting quite popular among the A-list celebrities. Just on Feb 2022, Cardi B retweeted her post.

Reportedly, Angeria began her drag journey on the 2012 world aids day drag show, and never looked back.

If you’re interested in her merch, find it at “angeriaqueen.com.” Her stickers, posters, and tees cost anywhere from $8 to $60.

Angeria Paris VanMicheals Family

Angeria didn’t talk much about her family. However, we’ve got a few names who she tagged as her relatives — Courtney Dallas (mother), Kamera Vanderpump (aunt), and Erykah Nyx (aunt).

But neither of them seemed to be related to her biologically.

Mentioning more of her drag family, Angeria has a big sister named Jaine Devereaux Van Micheals, a son named SaiVyon D. Van Micheals, and a daughter named London West.

Despite being anonymous, Angeria’s parents have made their appearance over her socials, and they seemed to be super supportive of her.

Trivia: Angeria’s best friend of 10 years, a drag queen Jeigh James passed away on Dec 27, 2021. 

Related FAQs

  • How Old Is Angeria Paris VanMicheals?

Angeria was born on July 25, 1993. That made her 28 years of age when she first appeared on RuPaul’s Drag Race in 2022.

  • Where Is Angeria Paris VanMicheals From?

She hails from Atlanta, GA, USA.

But since her hometown lacked fellow drag people, Angeria moved to Valdosta, GA, for college where she nurtured her passion. Finally, she then moved to Atlanta for her drag career.
