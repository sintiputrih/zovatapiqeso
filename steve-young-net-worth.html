Steve Young Biography

Steve Young (wuxuu dhashay Maarso 26, 1961) waa xirfadle dib-u-dhac ku soo noqday oo ka soo ciyaaray Horyaalka Kubadda Cagta Qaranka. Wuxuu ku dhashay San Francisco, California wuxuuna ka baxay Jaamacadda Brigham Young. Ka dib markii uu u ciyaaray kubbadda koleejka ee BYU Cougars, Young wuxuu u wareegay Kuliyadda Kaniisadda ee Hawaii hal xilli. Ka dib waxa uu ku soo laabtay BYU halkaas oo uu ku hogaaminayay muddo saddex sano ah iyaga oo ah bilawga bilawga ah oo u dejiyay diiwaanka dugsiga gabi ahaanba qayb kasta oo gudubta.

Dhalinyaradu waxay ahayd doorashadii guud ee ugu horeysay ee 1984 NFL Draft ee Tampa Bay Buccaneers. Wuxuu u sii ciyaaray Buccaneers (1984-1991), San Francisco 49ers (1992-1994), iyo San Diego Chargers (1995). Wuxuu ahaa Pro Bowler labadaba 1988 iyo 1990 wuxuuna sidoo kale kooxdiisa u horseeday guushii Super Bowl XXXII ee Denver Broncos, in kasta oo uusan ciyaarin ciyaarta.

Dhalinyaradu waxay dad badani u arkaan inuu yahay mid ka mid ah dib-u-dhacyadii ugu weynaa abid. Super Bowl XXIX, wuxuu ku lug lahaa mid ka mid ah soo laabashada ugu weyn abid, isaga oo dib uga soo celiyay kooxdiisii ​​28-dhibcood.
