Cheryl Burke is a professional dancer from San Francisco, CA, born on May 3, 1984. She is best known for her appearances on ABC's Dancing with the Stars, where she has been a professional dancer for over a dozen and a half seasons. She is the first professional to win the show, and also the first to win consecutive seasons.
