Jason Oppenheims new $300k car with 27-inch TV disappoints as fans call it ugly

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Jason Oppenheim, renowned for his Los Angeles mansions expertise and his appearance on the hit Netflix show “Selling Sunset,” recently unveiled his latest luxury sports car on Instagram. While Jason seems thrilled with his new wheels, not all of his fans share the same enthusiasm. Despite the car’s impressive features, including a built-in 27-inch TV, some fans have voiced their disappointment, particularly concerning its exterior design. In this article, we’ll take a closer look at Jason Oppenheim’s new vehicle and the reactions it has garnered from fans.

Jason’s New Car Revelation

Jason Oppenheim, the owner of The O Group and a familiar face from “Selling Sunset,” decided to share an exciting car update with his 1.4 million Instagram followers. He proudly announced that he would be among the first to own the cutting-edge FF 91 2.0 Futurist Alliance electric vehicle by Faraday Future.In a captivating cinematic reel, the Netflix star showcased the interior of the luxury SUV, complete with plush reclining massage seats and an array of high-tech gadgets. Notably, the car features a remarkable 27-inch television, adding a touch of entertainment luxury to the ride. After demonstrating the car’s impressive features, Jason donned his racing gear and hit the track, expressing his eagerness to push the vehicle to its limits.According to Engadget, this high-tech marvel carries an estimated price tag of around $309,000, making it a top-tier luxury vehicle.

Mixed Fan Reactions

While Jason’s new car received its fair share of praise from fans, there were also dissenting voices among his followers. Here’s a glimpse of the varied reactions:

  • One enthusiastic fan applauded the choice of an electric vehicle, emphasizing its importance in addressing climate change issues.
  • Another supporter marveled at the car, particularly highlighting the game-changing massaging seats.
  • However, not all fans shared the same enthusiasm. One critic described the car as “possibly the ugliest car I’ve seen this decade,” echoing a sentiment that it might be the “ugliest car ever.”
  • Another detractor appreciated the car’s technology but didn’t hold back in labeling its design as “ugly.”

Jason’s Relationship Update

In the latest season of “Selling Sunset,” viewers were introduced to Jason’s new girlfriend, Marie-Lou. Their relationship appeared to be flourishing as they embarked on European adventures together. However, Jason recently made a public announcement regarding their split.Taking to his Instagram, Jason disclosed that the challenges of a long-distance relationship proved insurmountable for the couple. Despite the separation, he emphasized that they remain close friends and hold each other’s best interests at heart. Jason’s ability to maintain amicable relationships with his exes, such as Mary Fitzgerald and Chrishell Stause, is a testament to his commitment to fostering positive connections.My Final Opinion, Jason Oppenheim’s new luxury car, with its impressive array of features, has sparked a range of reactions among his fans. While some celebrate the vehicle’s advanced technology and environmental consciousness, others are less enthused about its design. In the realm of relationships, Jason’s recent split with Marie-Lou demonstrates his capacity for maintaining respectful connections with his former partners.
