Who is the author of 4 Cut Hero?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 16, 2024

4 Cut Hero is a popular webtoon series that has captured the attention of many readers worldwide.

It is a fantasy and comedy genre webtoon that has garnered a lot of positive reviews due to its unique storytelling and well-written comedic elements.

About the Webtoon

4 Cut Hero is a webtoon created by Gojira-kun. The series is published by Lezhin Comics and has gained immense popularity due to its intriguing storyline and well-developed characters.

It is a story with a lot of worldbuilding and a well-written comedy element, making it an engaging read for fans of the genre.

Author Information

  • Author's Name: Gojira-kun
  • Genre: Fantasy, Comedy
  • Publisher: Lezhin Comics
  • Original Run: Ruliweb Creative Cartoon Board: 2011, Lezhin: August 19, 2014 -
  • Seasons: 2
  • Volumes: 4
  • Chapters: 216+

Art and Plot Analysis

Art

The art of 4 Cut Hero has been praised for its portrayal of attacks and meaningful action scenes. Despite being perceived as average by some, the art effectively conveys the force of impactful blows, earning it an art rating of 8/10.

Plot

The plot of 4 Cut Hero has been described as hard to follow due to numerous characters and extensive lore. This being said, it is rewarding to absorb, with detailed backstories that immerse readers into the story.

The webtoon is known for its unique blend of fantasy and modern elements, leading to a captivating and unconventional mix that keeps readers engaged.

Reviews and Recommendations

Positive Reviews

  • One reviewer praised the series for its intense fighting scenes, top-notch humor, and the right amount of fan service.
  • Another reviewer highlighted the amazing worldbuilding and lovable characters, emphasizing the creativity and use of side characters that make the story exceptional.

Negative Reviews

  • A reviewer expressed dissatisfaction with the series, criticizing it as random nonsense with lazy attempts at humor and unoriginality.

Anime Adaptation

Synopsis of the Anime

The 4 Cut Hero anime is a Chinese donghua that parodies harem culture while combining various genres, including action, adventure, comedy, and fantasy.

The plot revolves around a hero who finds himself facing unemployment and mediocrity after defeating the Demon King, leading to an unconventional mix of fantasy and modern elements with a peculiar hero at the forefront.

Cast and Availability

The anime features a sizable cast, and the first season aired on July 17, 2023, with ongoing episodes. It is based on the Korean webtoon series 4 Cut Yongsa by Gojira-kun and is a Chinese donghua animated program created by Studio Phantom, with Bilibili in charge of distribution.

Where to Watch

The anime is available for streaming on the Bilibili platform, and episodes are released every Monday. While Crunchyroll does not have the 4 Cut Hero anime, fans can access it on Bilibili's official YouTube channel, making it easily accessible to viewers in selected regions.

On the whole

The author of 4 Cut Hero, Gojira-kun, has created a compelling webtoon series that has garnered widespread attention for its unique storytelling, well-developed characters, and captivating blend of fantasy and comedy.

With an ongoing anime adaptation, the series continues to attract fans who enjoy immersive storytelling and engaging humor.

Gojira-kun's 4 Cut Hero has left a lasting impression on readers and viewers, solidifying its place as a standout work in the realm of webtoons and anime adaptations.
