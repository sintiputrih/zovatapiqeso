'The Bachelor' Season 27: Gabi Elnicki reveals her deepest insecurities to Zach Shallcross

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, February 14, 2024

KRABI, THAILAND: The final three women of 'The Bachelor' Season 27 are Ariel Frenkel, Gabi Elnicki and Kaity Biggar. Zach Shallcross will eliminate one of them from the show in the upcoming episode. 

In the upcoming episode, we will see the cast members of 'The Bachelor' flying to Krabi, Thailand. This episode will be emotional. ABC recently released a sneak peek clip of the upcoming episode, saying, "One thing I can promise you: It's going to be an emotional week." In the clip, we can see Gabi revealing she had a lot of insecurities. Keep reading to know more.

What is 'haunting' Gabi Elnicki?

In the recently released sneak peek of the upcoming episode, we see Zach and Gabi sitting on the beach. Gabi confesses to Zach, "Right now I'm feeling a lot of insecurity." She further says that she had some "feelings" which had been "haunting" her. She elaborates what has been haunting her this week. Gabi says, "So, I was in my head a little bit this week." Zach listens carefully to Gabi, and asks, "Just, cause of time?" Gabi replies, "It's more like, in previous relationships, I was like kind of chosen second. And, so not seeing you for so long, and being the second fantasy suite. I was like I feel very second again," she says. 

Did Zach Shallcross slam Gabi Elnicki instead of consoling her?

Instead of consoling Gabi, Zach seems to have slammed her by saying, "It's not a mind game, it's literally trying to figure out day by day. Just know it feels right to me." This did not help. She addresses her replies in a confessional. She says, "This is putting it my head. There are other women here. You just get in your own mind." Gabi stands up suddenly and says, 'I'll be right back," and leaves Zach alone. She is seen going into a corner, somewhere behind the trees and crying her heart out. The star says, "I feel very stupid and foolish and why can't I be happy?"

Will Gabi be eliminated? According to Reality Steve's spoilers, Ariel Frenkel is the one who will leave in the upcoming rose ceremony.

'The Bachelor' Season 27 airs every Monday on ABC at 8 pm ET.
