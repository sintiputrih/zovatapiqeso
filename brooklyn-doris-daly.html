Brooklyn Doris Daly- Meet Single Daughter Of Kenya Moore and Marc Daly

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 26, 2024

Celebrity daughter Brooklyn Doris Daly just grabbed the media attention since her birth. She was born to American actress Kenya Moore and her husband, Marc Daly, a businessperson. Kenya rose to fame after appearing in The Real Housewives of Atlanta from 2012 to 2018 and The Celebrity Apprentice 7 in 2015.

Doris was born on November 3, 2018, in the United States. Her grandparents are Ronald Grant and Patricia Moore. Today, let's talk about her in the below sections. 

Brooklyn's Personal Life

Brooklyn Daly has been gathering people's attention as the daughter of celebrity parents and has many fans following as well. Little girl Doris Daly who is still in her adolescence, shares a strong connection with her parents who adores her a lot.

Further, she is highly active on Instagram account with 221k followers, which is run by her mommy. She recently celebrated her 2nd birthday party on November 3, 2020, filled with "Moore Manor" festive decor. Her beautiful mom, Kenya, posted images and videos of her daughter's second birthday bash on her IG account.

She wrote: " Happy 2nd birthday my angel! You are my perfect gift from God. The unconditional love I’ve prayed for my entire life". She also added that: "This is just our beginning. The world is yours". So at the moment, Doris is in quarantine with her parents in Atlanta.

Parents' (Kenya and Marc) Marital Relationship

The Bravo star, Moore and the businessman, Daly have already parted the ways with one another in September 2019 after marrying for two years in June 2017. 

The love birds tied the knot on 10 June 2017 in an intimate ceremony. Their big day was held in a beachside ceremony in St. Lucia in the presence of their close friends and relatives. As per Moore, they first met in Brooklyn, New York.

As soon as they first met, the duo started hanging out, and after years of dating, they married. Unfortunately, the pair parted away from each other even though they share a kid. Now, Kenya says her ex-husband Marc is doing great to keep their intimacy alive.

Marc Reportdely Cheated On Kenya For His Ex

Yes, the restaurateur 50, has ended his two years of marriage with Kenya, 49, due to his inappropriate contact with his EX. Moore explained in an exclusive interview with the RHOA and with her best-friends, Cynthia Bailey and Kandi Burruss.

She caught her husband having intimate chats with one of his ex. She said that the woman was begging him to keep the sex going, even though he was married. After finding out about it, she confronted her partner, Marc, and the woman sending the texts. 

She even picked up her phone and told her not to come anywhere else near her husband. Now, Kenya, who won the Miss USA crown in 1993, states: " Marc is really fighting for his marriage, he’s made appointments". She also added he is asking to publicly apologize to her and a lot of things that she never thought she'd see the day.

Enjoys Parents (Kenya and Marc) Net Worth

Her mother, Kenya, the 1.78 m tall has a whopping net worth of $800,000, which she has earned from acting and modelling career. Officially, she began earning at the age of 22 when she won Miss Michigan USA in 1993 and became the second Afro-American woman to win Miss USA. 

She modelled for the Ebony Fashion Fair cosmetic line and many other reputed modelling companies. Then, she stepped forward in the world of showbiz industry and started acting. To date, she has contributed Hollywood with many hit movies such as I Know Who Killed Me, which earned $9,669,758 and Senseless, which has earned over $12,874,899 worldwide. 

So, the average salary of an actress is around $56,000 per year. However, being a well-known and one of the top-notched actress, her yearly salary must be over $100,000. In 2008, she has launched her own company named Moore Vision Media.

On the other hand, Marc has an estimated net worth of $1 Million. Most of that fortune came from his career as a restaurateur, broker and businessperson. He is the owner of the Brooklyn, New York's restaurant named SoCo, previously owned by Malissa Browne.
