Son of ex-basketball player Dave Holmgren, Chet Holmgren is an American basketball player who attends Minnehaha Academy in Minnesota (Minneapolis). Holmgren is a consensus five-star recruit and one of the highest-ranked players in the 2021 class plays the center position. Holmgren is known for his ball-handling and perimeter shooting skills.

Entering his junior season, Chet had about 30 scholarship offers from college basketball programs. According to ESPN, in June 2020, after the reclassification of Jonathan Kuminga, he became the number one player in the 2021 class.

Who Is Chet Holmgren Father? His Family And Parents Details
