Shoppers Say This $30 Oversized Cardigan Feels Like Wearing a Blanket

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, May 24, 2024

I don’t know about you, but every time fall rolls around, I have the urge to completely revamp my sweater collection. While that’s not entirely feasible, Amazon’s wide selection of affordable knits makes it possible to grab a few new styles. And the latest sweater on my wishlist is this Merokeety oversized cardigan that’s on sale starting at $30 with an on-site coupon. 

The longline cardigan is made from a “soft and cuddly” waffle-knit material, per a reviewer, and it comes in 22 colors, including classic neutrals and trendy autumnal hues. It has slouchy, drop-shoulder sleeves, side pockets, and an open-front, button-free silhouette. Plus, it’s machine-washable for a no-fuss laundry day, which is music to my dry-cleaning-hating ears. 

Cardigans are great styling pieces, since they take on a whole new look depending on what you wear underneath them. For casual days at home or running errands, throw on the cardigan over a graphic tee, add on a pair of leggings, and finish off the look with comfy sneakers or cozy Ugg boots. If you’re getting brunch with friends, style the cardigan with a simple tank or tee, high-waisted jeans, and ankle boots. And for the office, the sweater will look chic and sophisticated with a blouse, tailored trousers, and heeled booties. 

In the sweater’s Amazon reviews section, more than 2,000 people have given it a five-star rating. One shopper who owns the cardigan in three colorways said they’re “so cozy, yet not too heavy,” while someone else confirmed the sweater is “incredibly soft and snuggly and fits like the picture.” A third person even said it “feels like wearing a blanket.” 

Other shoppers commented on the cardigan’s fit and durability over time. A reviewer said it “hangs just right without clinging in all the wrong places,” and a second shopper said it “hits at just the right length.” Plus, another reviewer said the cardigan “gets softer and softer after every wash,” meaning you can wear it on repeat without worrying about it losing its cozy feel. 

If, like me, you’ve been convinced you need the Merokeety oversized cardigan, be sure to grab it while it’s on sale at Amazon, and check out more styles from the brand, below.
