Net Worth Of G Herb

G Herb has an estimated net worth around $3 million. The rapper is African-American born in 1995 from Ilinois. He is well-known for his debut mixtape, Welcome to Fazoland. He first started gaining recognition when he recorded songs with his cousin Lil Bibby and uploaded it to Youtube. He was also complimented by rapper Drake and went on to put out few mixtapes like Pistol P Project, Ballin Like I’m Kobe and Strictly 4 My Fans. His debut studio album was released in 2017 titled, Humble Beast. His hit singles are Red Snow, I Like, 4 Minutes of Hell, Pull Up, Drop, Yeah I Know, Ain’t Nothing to Me, XXL, Chiraq, Kill Shit, and so on.

G Herbo’s real name is Herbert Wright III and he was pled not guilty after he was charged for his involvement in a $1.5 million scheme in Massachusetts.
