Wiki/Biography & Career

Anuv Jain was born on Saturday, 11 March 1995 (age 25 years; as in 2020) in the Ludhiana city of Punjab. His zodiac sign is Pisces.

Anuv Jain as a Child with his Mother and Twin Sister

He was brought up in Ludhiana and pursued his BBA from Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies in Mumbai. In 2012, he opened his YouTube channel with an aim to make pure music; he was in class 11 at that time. After He uploaded his first song ‘Meri Baaton Me Tu’ in 2012.

After completing his graduation, he began working at a company named ZS Associates but later quit his job. A few of his popular songs include Ocean, Baarishein (also his first studio recorded song), Riha, and Maula.
