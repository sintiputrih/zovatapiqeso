Introduction :

  • Alec Bevere is an American filmmaker, director, writer and actor.
  • He is also the co-founder of the Sons & Daughters ministry, as well as the son of Lisa and John Bevere.

Personal Life, Parents and Family Details :

  • Alec was born Joshua Alexander Bevere on July 6, 1991, in Colorado, to parents John and Lisa Bevere, both authors and ministers.
  • Alec has three brothers: Austin Michael Bevere, Arden Christopher Bevere, and Addison David Bevere.
  • Alec was raised in Colorado, where he attended Pine Creek High School in Colorado Springs.
  • He proposed marriage to his partner at the time, in October 2015, but the engagement was broken shortly afterward, and Alec remains currently single. He has often talked about grief and heartbreak since the end of his engagement.
  • Alec is very close to his family and very passionate about ministry.
  • He continues to reside in his native Colorado and is currently attending University of Colorado Colorado Springs (UCCS).

Church Work : Income, Earnings and Net Worth

  • Alec founded the Sons and Daughters Ministry together with his brothers Arden Bevere and Addison Bevere. The ministry is branched off from their parents’ ministry, Messenger International.
  • Apart from his inherited passion for ministry, Alec is also an avid filmmaker.
  • He has written twelve shorts so far: Would You Ever Marry Someone Like This? (2015), Holding a Fence (2017), Poison (2017), Justin and Angela (2017), Knockoff Jesus (2017), The Cliff (2017), The Prisoner (2017), Pony Express (2017), The Final Stretch (2018), Frankly Speaking (2018), The King’s Decree (2018), and The Love You’re Looking For (2019).
  • Alec acted in most of the shorts he’s written, and he also directed The Love You’re Looking For. He produced The Prisoner.
  • In early 2019, Bevere launched his own film studio, Messenger Studios, again based on his parents’ ministry and created with their aid and blessing. Alec wishes to create films that speak of truth and ministry, which is why he wants to represent Messenger International in his own professional career.
