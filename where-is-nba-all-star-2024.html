Where is NBA All-Star 2024?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, April 2, 2024

The NBA All-Star 2024 event is set to take place in Indianapolis, Indiana. The 73rd NBA All-Star Game will be held on Sunday, Feb. 18, 2024, at Gainbridge Fieldhouse, the home of the Indiana Pacers. The State Farm All-Star Saturday Night, which includes the AT&T Slam Dunk, the Starry 3-Point Contest, and the Kia Skills Challenge, will be held at Lucas Oil Stadium on Saturday, Feb. 17. The All-Star weekend will span from Feb. 16-18, 2024, and numerous events and activities are planned to take place during this time.

History of NBA All-Star Games

Indianapolis previously hosted the NBA All-Star Game in 1985, where the Western Conference defeated the Eastern Conference 140-129 in the 35th All-Star Game. The crowd attendance of 43,146 at the Hoosier Dome marked the largest attendance for an NBA game at that time.

NBA All-Star 2025

The NBA All-Star 2025 will be hosted in San Francisco, Calif. from Feb. 14-16, 2025. The 74th All-Star game will be played on Sunday, Feb. 16, 2025, at Chase Center, home of the Golden State Warriors. On-court events will commence on Friday, Feb. 14, with the NBA Rising Stars showcase, followed by the NBA HBCU Classic and the State Farm All-Star Saturday Night featuring various competitions such as the Kia Skills Challenge, the Starry 3-Point Contest, and AT&T Slam Dunk.

NBA All-Star 2026

The NBA All-Star 2026 is scheduled to take place in Los Angeles, Calif. from Feb. 13-15, 2026. The 75th All-Star game will be played on Sunday, Feb. 15, 2026, at the Intuit Dome, the new home of the LA Clippers. The on-court events will kick off on Friday, Feb. 13, with the NBA Rising Stars showcase, followed by the State Farm All-Star Saturday Night featuring various competitions such as the Kia Skills Challenge, the Starry 3-Point Challenge, and AT&T Slam Dunk.

The schedule of events for the NBA All-Star 2024 in Indianapolis includes several exciting activities spread across the weekend. Some of the key events are as follows:

Friday, Feb. 16

  • Panini Rising Stars Practice at Gainbridge Fieldhouse.
  • Basketball Hall of Fame News Conference televised on NBA TV and NBA App.
  • Ruffles NBA All-Star Celebrity Game at Lucas Oil Stadium.
  • Panini Rising Stars featuring semifinals and final at Gainbridge Fieldhouse.

Saturday, Feb. 17

  • NBA All-Star Practice presented by AT&T at Gainbridge Fieldhouse.
  • HBCU Classic presented by AT&T featuring menâ€™s basketball programs from two historically Black colleges and universities at Gainbridge Fieldhouse.
  • State Farm All-Star Saturday Night at Lucas Oil Stadium, including the Kia Skills Challenge, the Starry 3-Point Contest, and the highly anticipated 'Stephen vs. Sabrina 3-Point Challenge', followed by the AT&T Slam Dunk competition.

Sunday, Feb. 18

  • NBA Legends Brunch televised on NBA TV and NBA App.
  • G League Up Next Game presented by AT&T at Indiana Convention Center.
  • 73rd NBA All-Star Game at Gainbridge Fieldhouse, featuring the East vs. West teams.

The NBA All-Star Weekend is expected to be packed with entertainment and basketball action, catering to fans of all ages.

The NBA All-Star 2024 will be held in Indianapolis, Indiana, at various venues such as Gainbridge Fieldhouse and Lucas Oil Stadium, offering a wide array of events and activities for fans to enjoy. The subsequent NBA All-Star Games in 2025 and 2026 are scheduled to take place in San Francisco, Calif., and Los Angeles, Calif., respectively, showcasing the continued tradition of this prestigious event.
