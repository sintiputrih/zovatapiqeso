Azari Sandlin, Amani Evans, Prince Youngblood!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 26, 2024

Meet Basketball Wives star Angel Brinks kids Azari Sandlin, Amani Evans, and Prince Youngblood in this article below. Angel recently talked to PageSix about Cardi B giving her a big career break. This also shifted attention from her career to the kids and the respective baby father.

So, keep scrolling down, as this article unfolds details on Angel Brinks’s Kids.

How Many Kids Does Basketball Wives Star Angel Brinks Have?

Basketball Wives Angel Brinks made her show debut in Season 4. A mother of three kids, she recently revealed to PageSix that Cardi B chose her so that she could prove herself as a fashion designer after being harshly picked by her co-stars in the show. Her co-stars picked on her because they didn’t take her seriously as a businesswoman and fashion designer. But, Cardi felt that she deserved much more respect than that.

Cardi then ended up wearing a sparkling outfit designed by Angel for her performance at the VMAs, as well as a second one for her appearance at the Floyd Mayweather vs. Connor McGregor fight.

Angel told to PageSix, “I will always remember that. It was her first very big performance and she came to me. So I just feel very blessed”.

She also dressed some of the big names in the entertainment industry such as Heidi Klum, Missy Elliot, Lil Kim, Doja Cat, Saweetie, and Megan Thee Stallion to name a few.

Besides her jobs, her relationship also put her in the limelight before. Now, she is a mother of three children in 2021. Learn more about her kids below in this article.

Meet Angel Brinks First Born, Azari Sandlin

Angel Brinks’s firstborn is a son named Azari. In 2016, Angel told Bossip, “He’d watch cartoons, see situations there and wonder ‘Where’s my daddy?” It is to be noted that Azari’s father died a while back.

“I’m not sure what’s harder — when fathers are in your life and leave or die, or never knowing them at all. When someone passes, at least we knew them and had them in our lives,” she went on. “As a parent, I struggle with that”.

  • Azari Sandlin Age

Azari is turning 15 years old on 18 July 2022. He is currently 14 years old in June 2022, at the time of the publishment of this article.

  • Who Is Azari Sandlin Father?

Angel Brinks’s son Azari was born from her first marriage with rapper Bruce Sandlin AKA X1. Sadly on 4 July 2007, just two weeks after her son was born, Bruce committed suicide. He was found dead in his Las Vegas home. He died at the age of 28.

Distractify reported that were married for only three months and had divorced before his death.

In 2016, Angel tweeted, “My ex-husband passed away when I was 34 weeks pregnant with my son. I had Azari two weeks later prematurely from the sadness and stress”.

X1 is a born and bred Brooklyner. He and his group of friends were discovered by the rap group Onyx and later signed by Gang Green. X1 was previously married to his ex-wife Baby Vasquez with whom he shared a son.

The ex-wife of X1, Vasquez, has alleged that her ex-husband didn’t commit suicide and his death was staged to make it look like a suicide. She claimed that he was a victim of homicide.

  • Is Azari Sandlin On Instagram?

Yes, Azari is on Instagram (@azthedon). But the profile doesn’t have any posts.

Meet Angel Brinks Second Born, Amani Evans

Amani Evans, is the beautiful second-born child, among three of Angel Brinks Kids.

On Amani’s birthday in August 2021, Angel posted on her IG, “My beautiful, smart, witty, sweet, helpful, loving, loyal, Leo, Amani. Happy birthday!!!! May God continue to bless you, and light the way! You leave so many imprints on others’ hearts. Such a humanitarian, animal lover, and overall good soul.”

She added, “Amani is the type that will stop and give her food to people without housing, and has no malice in her heart. You go with me everywhere and have become my best friend. Never even had terrible 2’s, and have been such a behaved child since day one. Having you has been one of my happiest moments in life. May you get everything you want out of life, and I will be the powerhouse behind it!! @amanievans”.

  • Amani Evans Age

Amani celebrates her birthday on 20 August. Born in 2013, Amani is 8 years old in June 2022.

  • Who Is Amani Evans Father?

Angel Brinks shared her daughter Amani with NBA player Tyreke Evans. Starting from 2010, they have been on and off. However, after the birth of Amani, they moved separate ways.

  • Is Amani Evans On Instagram?

Yes, Amani is on Instagram (@amanievans).

Meet Angel Brinks Third Born, Prince Youngblood

Angel Brinks welcomed her third born named Prince in November 2021. She announced a new baby via an IG post back on 19 October 2021. The was to celebrate her birthday and also announce that she was welcoming her third child. She captioned the post, “It’s my birthday. So thankful for family, love, success … and speaking of birth, it’s almost time! More surprises on the way.”

  • Prince Youngblood Age

In June 2022, Prince is currently 7 months old.

  • Who Is Prince Youngblood Father?

Angel welcomed her third baby with RoccStar, born Leon Youngblood Jr. RoccStar, after the birth of their son, took to Instagram and wrote, “Thank you @angelbrinks for bringing into the world the greatest gift ever! A young king and a prince!”.

Angel and RoccStar reportedly started dating each other in late 2020. They have made a public appearance together a few times. But, besides sharing a son, they also have a business named RoccStar’s Angels Trucking, LLC. The company is based in North Hills, California.

RoccStar has also made an appearance on Basketball Wives where the parents of Prince discussed her pregnancy previously. The music producer also has a son named Syhre Leon Youngblood from his relationship with her ex, singer Kris Stephens.

More about RoccStar, he is a songwriter, producer, rapper, and more. He has worked with music artists such as Chris Brown, Post Malone, Usher, Rita Ora, Fergie, and more.

  • Is Prince Youngblood On Instagram?

No, Prince doesn’t have an Instagram account yet.
