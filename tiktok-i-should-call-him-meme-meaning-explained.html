TikTok: I Should Call Him Meme Meaning Explained

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, February 16, 2024

I Should Call Him TikTok meme is getting so popular amid the COVID-19 pandemic. While many of us have to spend our lives indoors during this crisis, we are looking for ways to entertain ourselves. Don’t we? Besides using the internet, what could be there to ease our loneliness?

In the name of providing entertainment, people have begun sharing memes on social media regularly these days. While memes have been simple means of sharing humor throughout social media, it has now become our quarantine mood. As we try to adjust this new livelihood, memes about this pandemic itself have started surfacing on social media. 

Recently TikTok has the best quarantine mood meme. If you just the social media app regularly, you might have come across this meme ‘I Should Call Him.’ Well, if we can’t meet in person, all you can do is dial the number on the phone and talk, isn’t it?

So, what actually is ‘I Should Call Him’ TikTok meme? Let’s find out.

I Should Call Him TikTok Meme Explained

Memes were just a mere tool to share humor though social media like Facebook or Twitter. But with changing times, the definition of the meme has come to differ, as well. Today, it has become a mode of communication than just making us laugh. I Should Call Him TikTok meme is also just more than a mere tool of laughter.

You miss someone, what you will do? Obviously, you will call him/her and go meet the person. But, these days, it is tough to reach the persona we miss in person. So, all the option we have left is to call them. I Should Call Him TikTok meme is kind of similar. 

Several people have been using this hashtag on TikTok to make videos like every day. You are doing something but is not able to execute the work properly, you suddenly think of someone who can perform the work properly. Then you just call that person. TikTok is also using this theme to make TikTok videos under hashtag I Should Call Him. 

On May 15, a TikToker uploaded a TikTok video using the aforementioned theme. He was applying some makeup lotion on his face, but can’t do it properly. Then he says ‘I Should Call Him.

So, now you know how people are using I Should Call Him meme on TikTok.

Is I Should Call Him meme trending well on TikTok?

TikTok users have made their way through several popular memes. And I Should Call Him is one of the most popular memes on TikTok this time around. If you search for a hashtag I Should Call Him on TikTok, it has 17.8 million TikTok video views.

Moreover, simply calls him hashtag on TikTok has 37.5 million video views. Well, it is not tough to figure out how popular is I Should Call Him meme ono TikTok.

Several TikTok videos made using this hashtag has millions of views and thousands of comments. Well, this meme is not only popular on TikTok, but also has thousands of viewers on other social media apps like Facebook.

Is I Should Call Him meme popular on Facebook?

Memes have been an inevitable part of social media like TikTok and Facebook. While, we have seen the hype of I Should Call Him meme on TikTok, social media users are equally using this meme on other social media like Facebook.

Along with ‘Tasty These All Are Cakes’ meme, I Should Call Him meme is also popular on Facebook. There are several posts on Facebook under this meme. This meme is also popular on Reddit.
