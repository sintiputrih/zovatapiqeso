Biography:

Bridget Regan was born on February 3, 1982 in San Diego County, California, U.S.. She became passionate in theater at an early age and eventually attended University of North Carolina School of the Arts. Her career on television and film began in 2006, appearing in the short film Blinders, followed by the movies The Babysitters and Sex and the City.

On television, she made various guest appearances in the mid to late 2000s, before gaining attention for her role in Legend of the Seeker. She received further stardom for her character in White Collar. She now plays Rose in Jane the Virgin and Dottie Underwood in Agent Carter. She has recently appeared in the 2014 film John Wick.
