Adam Wainwright Relationship With Jenny Curry

In his high school days, Adam had a girlfriend whose name was Jenny Curry. They both had a strong bond. And in 2004 A.D. Adam married Jenny at the age of 28, since then they have lived together.

Talking about his beloved wife Jenny completed her study on Interior Designing from the Georgia Southern University. Adam and Jenny have three children. Morgan, Bailey, and Macy.

Whenever Adam used to have free time he used to spend all the time with his family. Having a long-lasting relationship Adam and Jenny never separated from each other.

Who is Caleb ?

Despite having three children Adam also has a son whose name is Caleb Adam Wainwright. Adam Wainwright was born on February 8, 2019, who was adopted by Adam and Jenny.

Adam and Jenny announced the adoption of the child in April 2019 after the two months birth of Caleb. Adam and Jenny were really happy with the adaption of the child and never let Caleb experience that he was an adopted child.
