Gabrielle Union &amp; Tia Mowry's Daughters Delight Fans with Cute Facial Expressions Riding a Car

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, May 15, 2024

Gabrielle Union and Tia Mowry's little girls had fans drooling with admiration when they met up for a previous play date. The girls are the sweetest besties.

TV stars Gabrielle Union and Tia Mowry's little daughters, Kaavia James and Cairo, are presently the cutest besties on the internet. The pair met up this past weekend for a little hangout.

Movie star Union shared a clip of the girls exploring their friendship, enjoying each other's company, and warming the hearts of many on her Instagram timeline.

Gabrielle Union and her daughter Kaavia Wade at Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Sports 2019 on July 11, 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

The clip opened with Kaavia and Cairo cooped up in Kaavia's white car and taking a ride around. Kaavia took some reverses, making her and Cairo giggle before they decided they were done riding.

The clip showed the tots getting out of the miniature Mercedes and making their way to the playground. The location of the post made it known that the little girls were on a playdate connection. Union wrote in her caption with some laughing emojis:

"The playdate we've all been waiting for… when @kaaviajames met Cairo!!! Bringing the girls together was magic and pure sweetness…"

Many Instagram users were excited to see the "two shadiest babies" together finally. One person shared a heart-eye emoji noting that Kaavia and Cairo had cool personalities.

More fans reeled with laughter, loving every moment documented in the clip. Star mom Mowry, also joined in the interaction, noting that their play date was the best time ever.

The doting mom fawned over her daugher, noting that she recognized words without the pictures.

A fan's comment on Kaavia and Cairo's video of them driving a car. | Instagram/gabunion

Since she was welcomed into the Union-Wade family, Kaavia has proven that she was born a star and has successful star parents to learn from. From her viral memes to sweet toddler moments, the little girl has her admirers eating right out of her tiny hands.

Earlier this year, her parents teased their new family project, which was solely inspired by her. In a recent interview, Union and Dwyane Wade made it known that the book "The Shady Baby" was their way of honoring little Kaavia.

A fan's comment on Kaavia and Cairo's video of them driving a car. | Instagram/gabunion

The book centers around the little girl who earned the "The Shady Baby" alias and uses her smartness to teach kids important values. It revolves around Kaavia's playtime and some of her adventures.

The celebrity couple shared that the book was geared to help little Black boys and girls see themselves in fairy tale stories, while they, as Kaavia's parents, put in effort in making the "The Shady Baby" title more positive.

Little Cairo has also been garnering a slew of admirers with her serious facial expressions and smart moves. Once in an interview, Mowry could not get enough of the little girl.

The doting mom fawned over her daugher, noting that she recognized words without the pictures and was just 17 months at the time. Mowry added that her baby was a calm child who makes no fuss.
