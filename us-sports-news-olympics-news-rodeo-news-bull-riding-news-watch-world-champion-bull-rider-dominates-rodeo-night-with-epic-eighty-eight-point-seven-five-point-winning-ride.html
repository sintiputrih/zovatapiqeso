Watch: World Champion Bull Rider Dominates Rodeo Night With Epic 88.75 PointWinning Ride

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 1, 2024

In the world of rodeo, a legend has captivated audiences with each daring ride. His transformation into a seasoned bull riding champion from a young and aspirational cowboy has been nothing short of amazing. Born into a family deeply rooted in the world of rodeo, he inherited a passion for the sport that would fuel his rise to greatness.

A story that started out with modest beginnings and eventually led to him becoming the 2022 PBR World Champion. In 2022, a year to remember, he showcased his grit and determination in a nail-biting competition that kept the audience on the edge of their seats. Recently, a video showing his thrilling victory over the powerful bull Hoka Hey surfaced on YouTube.

Bull Rider dominates rodeo might with a spectacular ride

In a recent YouTube video posted by PBR, Daylon Swearingen, the reigning World Champion Bull Rider, showcased an awe-inspiring performance. He fearlessly rode the fierce bull, Hoka Hey, for over eight seconds during Game 2 on Day 1 of the 2023 PBR Teams Series Outlaw Days in Kansas City, MO. He scored an astounding 88.75 points for the exhilarating ride, thrilling the crowd. What made the moment even more special was the presence of Daylon’s proud mother, who could be seen in the crowd cheering him on and recording every second of his incredible feat on her iPad.

Daylon’s unbeatable talent and constant dedication captivated viewers all over the world. The video provided a rare behind-the-scenes look at the world of professional bull riders. His 88.75-point winning ride on Hoka Hey during the 2023 PBR Teams Series Outlaw Days in Kansas City, MO, catapulted him into the limelight once again. His skills were put on full display in the PBR Team Series, a ground-breaking competition format that turned bull riding into a team sport.

His team, the Kansas City Outlaws, served as the event’s host while navigating the league’s inaugural season with fierce determination and a cast of coaches and bull riders.
