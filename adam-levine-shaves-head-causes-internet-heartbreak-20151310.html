Adam Levine Shaves Head, Causes Internet Heartbreak

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, March 21, 2024

Oh, Adam, why? During The Voice on Monday, Oct. 12, Adam Levine debuted a new look — a shaved head — that sent the Internet reeling with sorrow.

Once fans saw that the Maroon 5 frontman had no hair, they, well, freaked out. Levine’s good friend and fellow judge on the show, Blake Shelton, also chimed in on the singer’s new look.

“Thank goodness #Gandhi was there… #adamsbald,” the country superstar, 39, joked on Twitter.

In response, the “Moves Like Jagger” singer, 36, took to Twitter to address the issue of his bald head and all the commotion surrounding it.

“Excuse me,” the rocker tweeted. “Lex Luthor was bald. I have ‘shaved’ my head. Meaning, shaved by choice. Big difference. OK?!! OK?!!”
