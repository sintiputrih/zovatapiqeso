Jennifer Lopez Wore a Low-Cut Crop Top with Matching Chunky Sneakers

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, May 4, 2024

Jennifer Lopez is continuing her winning fashion streak of summer whites, this time with a low-cut white crop top and a matching pair of chunky sneakers.

J.Lo was photographed in Los Angeles on Tuesday with her daughter Emme, wearing the ribbed top with a pair of baggy pants rolled up at the ankles. She accessorized with large gold hoop earrings and gold necklaces, as well as stacked bracelets, a pair of rectangular sunglasses, a crossbody bag, and The Kooples sneakers.

The ensemble was a '90s spin on Lopez's recent go-to color scheme, an easy-breezy palette of whites and neutrals. From her plunging white maxi dress to the one-shouldered white gown she styled with a shoulder bracelet, J.Lo has been embracing crisp summer whites.

Lopez recently spoke of her current happiness amid her renewed relationship with Ben Affleck and her new music.

"Things happen that you don't expect to ever happen," she told Apple Music 1's Ebro Darden. "Once you get good. Once you get to the point where you're like, 'This is not right for me, or this doesn't feel good, or I need to make an adjustment here. This is not really about anybody else but me.' Once you do that, stuff starts falling into place."
