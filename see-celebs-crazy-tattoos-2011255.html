See Celebs' Wildest and Craziest Tattoos of All Time: From Grimes to Machine Gun Kelly

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 9, 2024

When it comes to tattoos, celebs aren’t afraid to take a walk on the wild side. 

While some stars like Hailey Bieber or Vanessa Hudgens are partial to dainty symbols and barely-there designs, others like to go all out. Grimes, for her part, got a back tattoo inspired by her fascination with aliens.

“Full back in white ink by @tweakt with some help form the alien computer brain of @nusi_guero,” she wrote via Instagram in April 2021. “Don’t have a good pic cuz it hurts too much and I need to sleep haha, and it’ll be red for a few weeks.”

Machine Gun Kelly, meanwhile, is no stranger when it comes to tattoos. However, the singer took fans by surprise in April 2021 when he got a bloody-looking slash down the center of his neck.
