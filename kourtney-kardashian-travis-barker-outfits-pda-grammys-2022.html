Kourtney Kardashian &amp; Travis Barker Coordinate Looks for Grammys

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, April 22, 2024

Kourtney Kardashian and fiancé Travis Barker have again graced us with their presence on a red carpet.

The couple coordinated in glam-rocker looks at the 2022 Grammys in Las Vegas, with Kardashian wearing a black jumpsuit that flared at the bottom and featured waist cutouts from Et Ochs's fall/winter 2022 collection, along with black sheer gloves and matching heels. Her hair was pulled back into a ponytail. Meanwhile, the Blink-182 drummer looked cool in a hot pink coat worn over a black Givenchy vest, pants, and shoes.

Frazer Harrison//Getty Images

Just like they did at the Oscars last month, the stars—a.k.a. Kravis—showed plenty of PDA in front of the cameras, and in red-carpet photos, Kardashian is seen gazing lovingly at Barker.

Frazer Harrison//Getty Images

At one point, the pair showed off their matching styles by each putting on dark sunglasses and posing hand in hand. Barker's shades were The Upsetter Sun pair in black from the Enfants Riches Déprimés x Jacques Marie Mage collaboration.

Frazer Harrison//Getty Images

At the Oscars, the Poosh founder and her husband-to-be walked the red carpet in matching black looks. Kardashian wore a strapless black dress by Thierry Mugler, a clutch bag, Aquazzura sandals, Lorraine Schwartz jewelry, and slicked-back hair, while Barker—who performed at the ceremony—looked dapper in a black suit and sunglasses.

Frazer Harrison//Getty Images

On social media, Kim Kardashian fangirled over the couple's appearance at the awards show. "This is so iconic," the SKIMS founder wrote over a photo of her sister and Barker at the event, along with a string of crying emojis. She also praised her future brother-in-law for his performance that night. "@travisbarker on the drums #Oscars," she wrote over a shot of Barker rocking out at the ceremony.
