Vin Di Bona Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, February 19, 2024
Vincent John “Vin” Di Bona (born 1944) is a television producer who has worked on a variety of American television shows, including America’s Funniest People, MacGyver, and Entertainment Tonight, as well as America’s Funniest Home Videos. He has also worked on many international television shows, including America’s Funniest Home Videos. Vin Di Bona Productions, which bears his name, is a production business he founded. In 2010, Di Bona started a second firm, FishBowl Worldwide Media, which is an independent production company that creates properties for cinema, television, digital platforms, and brands. FishBowl Worldwide Media is based in Los Angeles.
