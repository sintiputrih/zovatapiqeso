Bella Hadid flaunts her incredible figure in a Playboy bunny tankini

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

Bella Hadid was proudly flaunting her supermodel physique in a racy brown tankini in a slew of scantily-clad snaps on Monday. 

The supermodel, 24, strutted her stuff at the beach in an eye-catching two-piece embroidered with silver rhinestones recreating the famous Playboy Bunny logo in the new series of photos shared to her Instagram grid. 

Bella showcased her carefree side in the impromptu photo shoot, thought was tight-lipped on where she’d flown to during her surprise beach break.

Donning a quirky red, brown and yellow bucket hat, Bella flashed her million-dollar smile for the camera.

The beauty’s sun-kissed skin glowed against a brown faux suede tankini with rhinestone adornments and a crystal Playboy bunny logo. 

Her slim waist peaked out beneath the tank and Bella looked to have legs for days in the skimpy matching thong bottoms that sat high on her trim hips.

The setting for her pictures appeared to be a private tropical beach with not another human in sight and turquoise waves crashing in the background.
