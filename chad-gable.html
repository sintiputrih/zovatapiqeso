Chad Gable - Bio, Career, Net Worth, Height, Married, Wiki, Facts

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, January 31, 2024

Table of Biography

Chad Gable is a talented professional American wrestler who performs on the Raw brand and is signed with WWE. Chad Gable is also famous for his amateur wrestling career where he became the Minnesota State Wrestling Champion in 2004 and the U.S. Olympic Trials Champion in 2012.

Early Life and Education

Chad Gable was born on 8 March 1986 and he is currently 36 years old. He hails from Saint Michael, Minnesota, and his zodiac sign is Pisces.

Talking about his educational journey, Gable is an alumnus of Northern Michigan University. Likewise, Gable earned his master’s degree from Full Sail University in October 2021.

Talking about his family, his father’s name is Pete Betts and his mother is Pam Betts. Further, his full name is Charles Edward Betts. Additionally, this personality is a member of the Comanche Nation.

Chad Gable – Net Worth 2023

This professional American wrestler has earned a decent amount of money throughout his wrestling career. Moving on, this wrestler has a net worth of around $1.8 million US dollars.

Body Measurements

Chad Gable stands tall at an approximate height of 5 feet 8 inches or 173 cm and his weight is around 92 kgs or 202 lbs.

Likewise, Gable has brown hair and green eyes. Besides these, the wrestler’s other body measurement details are still under review.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Chad Gable is a wrestler. At present, Gable is signed to WWE. At WWE, this wrestler performs on the Raw brand under the ring name “Chad Gable”.

Furthermore, this wrestler is a 4-time tag team champion in WWE.

As an amateur wrestler in the past, Gable competed at the 2012 Summer Olympics in London. As such, this wrestler signed with WWE in late 2013.

Subsequently, this personality underwent training and later was deployed to the developmental branch NXT.

Talking more about his amateur wrestling career, he was the Minnesota State Wrestling Champion in the year 2004. Likewise, he won 4th place in the 2003 Minnesota State Wrestling.

These two were his accomplishments during this high school. Regarding his international medals, Gable won silver at the 2006 World University Games, 2012 Gedza International, and 2012 Pan-American Olympic Qualifier.

Similarly, he won gold medals at the 2012 Pan-American Championships and the 2012 Dave Schultz Memorial International.

Besides these, Gable also won bronze at the 2012 Granma Cup. Furthermore, Gable also became the U.S. Olympic Trials Champion in the year 2012.

More Details

Also, Gable is a tag team specialist. As such, Gable won the NXT Tag Team Championship with his partner Jason Jordan as American Alpha.

This was before being called up to the main roster in the year 2016. There, Jason and Gable won the SmackDown Tag Team Championship. However, the duo disbanded in mid-2017.

Later on, this wrestler formed a tag team with Shelton Benjamin. This was before being traded to Raw in the 2018 Superstar Shake-up.

Following this trade, Gable formed a team with Bobby Roode. The duo subsequently won the Raw Tag Team Championship in December 2018.

With this win, this wrestler became the 2nd man to hold all the NXT, Raw, and SmackDown Tag Team Championships.

The 1st wrestler to hold all these championships was Gable’s former tag team partner Jason Jordan. Recently in January 2022, this wrestler won the Raw Tag Team Championship for the 2nd time.

This time, it was with his tag partner – Otis (Nikola Bogojevic).

Additionally, this wrestler made his video game debut as a playable character in WWE 2K17. Following this, Gable appeared in WWE 2K18, WWE 2K19, WWE 2K20, and WWE 2K22.

Relationship Status

Chad Gable is a happily married lad. His wife’s name is Kristi Oliver.

The two are high school sweethearts and dated for 9 years before finally tying the knot on 19 June 2011. The lovely couple together has three children namely Clay, Ann, and Meadow.
