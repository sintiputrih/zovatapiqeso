Gigi Hadid Parties in a Leather Maxi Skirt and Fuzzy Sweater

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, April 28, 2024

Gigi Hadid is back with another perfectly cozy and chic party look.

The founder of knitwear brand Guest in Residence has become known for her ability to seamlessly mix comfort and style when it comes to dressing, and her latest outfit is further proof.

Hadid was photographed on her way to her new flagship store, opening in New York City’s NoHo neighborhood. For the busy day, she opted for a fuzzy white sweater with ribbed details at the neckline and sleeves (from Guest in Residence, of course), paired with a cool black leather maxi skirt from DL1961 that is sure to live on our mood boards for years to come. The sleek piece featured a high slit in the front, and Hadid cinched it at the waist with a black leather belt with a golden buckle.

“My goal in life is to look as chic or put together as possible while always being comfortable,” Hadid told Bazaar.com last year.

Raymond Hall//Getty Images

MEGA//Getty Images

The model finished the look with pointy black knee-high boots in leather and suede with kitten heels. For accessories, she kept it classic with chunky gold hoop earrings, gold rings, and a gold chain necklace. She wore a short white manicure and natural rosy makeup; her hair was styled in a casual, wavy half-up, half-down ’do.

“We wanted this to feel like someone’s living room,” Hadid said of her new store, per an Instagram post shared by the brand. “An easy place to come shop, hang, play chess in the back. It’s warm, it’s cozy, and it’s getting cold in the city, so come see us.”
