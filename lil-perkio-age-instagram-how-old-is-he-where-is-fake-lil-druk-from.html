Rapper Lil Prekio is currently in the people’s eyes after another rapper, 6ix9ine, posted a video. Here’s what we know about the incident that happened between Lil Durk look-alike and 6ix9ine.

Lil Prekio is a rapper from the United States of America who gradually gained fame in the Hip Hop scene. The young rapper, Prekio has become known for his striking resemblance to Lil Durk. And now, people are searching about the professional life of Prekio after his recent clip with 6ix9ine went viral.

The young rapper claimed that he was ambushed by 6ix9ine and his crew. So, Prekio said he was uncomfortable with the situation and didn’t want any part of the Lil Durk trolling session that 6ix9ine was looking to achieve. Recently, Prekio shared another video about the incident where he talked about everything we could find on his Instagram account.
