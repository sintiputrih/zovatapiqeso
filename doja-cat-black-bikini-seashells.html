Doja Cat Vacations in a Black Bikini Embellished With Dangling Seashells

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Doja Cat is inspiring all of our summer vacation wardrobes with a new set of sultry looks.

On Instagram yesterday, the singer shared a carousel of photos featuring looks by the water during a getaway trip to Los Cabos, Mexico. In two of the photos, the singer flaunts her figure aboard a boat while in a black bikini featuring a string of dangling seashells, which she pairs with dainty gold jewelry, alongside minimal makeup and a platinum-bleached pixie hairstyle. (She debuted the drastic hair shade earlier in the spring.) She topped off the look with a pair of black-rimmed green reflective oval shades.

Doja Cat / Instagram//Instagram

Doja Cat / Instagram//Instagram

In the rest of the photos, Doja poses by the beach wearing a sheer white-lace co-ord featuring a deep-V long-sleeved blouse and a matching ruffled low-rise miniskirt. She topped off the summery boho look with woven open-toe heeled mules, a pair of statement gold bangle earrings, and layers of gold necklaces.

Concluding the carousel of vacation looks, Doja signed off with a final close-up of herself making a face, a signature move that often shows up in the rapper’s social media posts.

The Grammy winner regularly takes to social media to share her latest looks with fans. For instance, Doja posted another fashion-filled Instagram photo dump last week. Then, she dressed in a red-and-tan graphic minidress featuring circular cutouts at the torso and bust.

Her style also reached new heights at this year’s Met Gala, where she opted to transform into the honored Karl Lagerfeld’s beloved cat via a sparkling hooded gown by Oscar de la Renta. “I use fashion to express myself,” she explained of the evening’s viral look. “So bottom line, any time that I’m doing something like this that’s avant-garde or out of the ordinary, it’s another way of just me expressing myself.”
