Freeda Foreman Wiki, Age, Husband, Biography, Family, Height &amp; More

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, March 3, 2024

Freeda Foreman (born 16 October 1976) is an American Professional Boxer, Actress, Voice Artist, and Athlete from Houston, Texas. She is popularly known as George Foreman’s Daughter (American Legendary Boxer).

The 42-years-old has also appeared in many Documentary Films like Beyond the Glory (2004), 10 Things Every Guy Should Experience (2004), and Relatively Speaking: George Foreman (2002).

She has also acted as Voice Actress for Family Guy (American sitcom) in 2003. Later in the same year, Foreman has played herself in King of the Hill Animated series.

Unfortunately, Freeda has died at the age of 42 in her house at Houston on 9 March 2019. The main cause of her death is her sudden heart attack.

Boxing Matches Records

  • She has played her debut boxing match against her opponent Laquanda Landers on 18 June 2000. The venue of this match is the Regent Hotel & Casino in Las Vegas.

  • In the same year, she has also fought her 6th boxing match against Evelyn Rodriguez on 20 November 2001 at Civic Center, Humble. In this match, Evelyn defeated her by the score. After this, Foreman has taken her retirement from this sports.
