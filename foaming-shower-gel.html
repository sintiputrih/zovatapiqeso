Highest-Rated Foaming Shower Gel of 2023 Hollywood Life Reviews Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, May 6, 2024

Taking a quick shower isn't really "quick" anymore. With the amazing beauty products currently on the market, taking showers is more than a cleansing ritual now, it can be soothing, it can be stress-relieving, and it can be luxurious. Yes, you can have a spa-like experience right in your bathroom.

Shower gels are in high demand now because of their rich and creamy lather that feels as though you were being caressed by cotton candy. It is more moisturizing than soaps and body washes and has more hydrating agents that keep your skin looking soft and supple. The best part? It takes only a little amount to get you to the foam and lather zone. Curious as to how to get the best foaming shower gel? Fret not! In this guide, we will talk more about the benefits, what to look out for and how to use it. Let's get started.

Why Do I Need a Shower Gel?

Keeps your skin hydrated

The secret to having healthy glowing skin is hydration. When your skin is moisturized it becomes soft, youthful, and healthy. Some regular bar soaps are often made with skin irritants that leave your skin feeling rough and dry after taking a bath.

Shower gels are different, they are milder and most of them are made with skin-friendly ingredients that have moisturizing and hydrating properties. Some shower gels usually have a rich creamy lather that leaves your skin feeling smooth and soft. It is suitable for use on your entire body and some are mild enough to be used on the face. However, we suggest that if you just use shower gels and body washes on the face, you should go for one with organic ingredients that are hypoallergenic.

Exfoliation 

Exfoliating your skin simply means getting rid of the dead skin cells so that new healthy ones can come out. Some shower gels have exfoliating properties to help rid your skin of ashy dry skin cells. 

Most shower gels with exfoliating properties are made from natural ingredients that are suitable even for sensitive skin types. It gently cleanses and removes impurities that have accumulated on your skin over time.

Travel-friendly

Shower gels come in beautiful containers that are mess-free and easy to carry. You don’t have to worry about how you’d take your skincare products with you on travels as you would a bath soap.

Shower gels are available in different size, pick a compact size and take it with you anywhere you go.

Great lather

When compared to soaps, shower gels give off a rich and creamy lather that moisturizes and nourishes your skin leaving it smooth and soft during and after baths. Go for gels with moisturizing ingredients for a more luxurious bathing experience.

How Do I Choose the Right Shower Gels?

Mild ingredients 

Your skin is a sensitive organ and can easily get irritated by harsh chemicals, especially if you have sensitive skin. Mang soaps and body washes contain ingredients that could possibly irritate your skin.

When buying shower gels, take your time to look over the ingredients. Ensure that it is clearly stated that it does not contain any dye, sulfates, parabens, or other chemicals. 

Choose a shower that is gentle and mild, since you would be using it daily, you don't want a product that would leave your skin looking dry or inflamed. Hypoallergenic formula is always your best bet to avoid skin irritations.

Moisturizing properties

For your skin to look great, and to remain healthy, you need a shower gel with moisturizing Properties inclusive in its ingredients. Some bar soaps and body washes remove your skin’s healthy oils, leaving it dry and prone to skin problems.

When choosing the right shower gel, ensure that it is rich in hydrating and conditioning agents that will help your skin remain smooth and soft. Well-moisturized shower gels can help your skin retain its moisture without further applications of moisturizers after taking a shower.

Fragrance

This is an important factor to consider when choosing the best shower gel for you. Showering shouldn’t just be about getting clean, it can be therapeutic too. And one of the key things to having a relaxing shower is the fragrance of the product being used.

Be very meticulous with choosing the right scent for you, pick one that you love, one that makes you want to close your eyes and drift to sleep. One that tantalizes your senses.

If you pick a shower gel with a fragrance that is too harsh or too strong, you may not only hate using it but it could also cause potential harm to your skin. However, if you have really sensitive skin, we suggest that you use shower gels that are fragrance-free or have a mild scent.

How Can I Use a Foaming Shower Gel?

You want to start by getting wet, duh! Gently rinse off your body, you may want to use warm water, but cold water works too. If you intend to wash your hair as well, then we suggest that you should wash your hair first. Why? It’s simple. To avoid dirt, hair follicles, and oily residue from being on your skin when you shower before washing your hair.

Next would be to use a small quantity of gel on a wet sponge, loofah, or washcloth, this will help you get a rich lather. Scrub gently on your skin. If you want to use your hands instead of a sponge, you would have to use more of the shower gel to get the desired lather or foam you want.

Gently work your way from top to bottom, using small, circular motions. You can let it stay on your skin for a couple of minutes, so you bask in the softness and fragrance. Rinse off after warm water and dry.

If you intend to use your shower gel in the bathtub for a more luxurious spa-like experience, you can add more product under running water to create a bubble, sit in it and scrub as explained. Make sure that the shower gel you use is mild and has a fantastic fragrance that speaks to your senses.
