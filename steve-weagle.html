Steve Weagle is the chief meteorologist of WPTV-TV based in West Palm Beach, Florida.

He is a CBM Seal holder from the American Meteorologists Society and is counted as one of the A-listed meteorologists in America. 

He has been in the field for over two decades as of 2020. 

Weagle’s career highlights include 2004 Hurricane Frances, Hurricane Jeanne, Hurricane Wilma, and the tornado of 2003, bringing destruction in South Florida.

He is very responsible and has contributed most during the disasters by conveying reliable information. 

Steve is also a good-hearted man who has been cycling 125 miles from Sebastian to Boca Raton every year for two decades under Steve’s Ride for the Red Cross. 

He has also collaborated with many local charities and speaks to thousands of people regarding preparedness for hurricanes.
