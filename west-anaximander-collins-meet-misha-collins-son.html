West Anaximander Collins: Meet Misha Collins son

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, February 17, 2024

Misha Collins has been appearing in films since 2005.

He’s also a philanthropist.

When he was cast as the angel Castiel in the blockbuster fantasy horror series Supernatural, his rise to celebrity was hastened.

He’s also acted in films including Karla and Girl, Interrupted, as well as TV shows like Charmed.

He has been a member of the supernatural cast since his debut as Jack Kline, Lucifer’s son, in the Season 12 finale.

Misha needs to be rewarded for his outstanding on-screen abilities as well as for making us laugh till our stomachs hurt.

This article, on the other hand, focuses on his son West Anaimander Collins.

West Anaximander Collins: Meet Misha Collins son

West Anaximander is the first child of the actor since his October 6, 2001, marriage to Victoria Vantoch in Maine.

West Anaximander Collins was born on September 23, 2010.

He had a daughter, Maison Marie Collins who was also born on September 25, 2012.
