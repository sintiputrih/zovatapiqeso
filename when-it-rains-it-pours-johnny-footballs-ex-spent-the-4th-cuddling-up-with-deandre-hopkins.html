When It Rains It Pours...Johnny Football's Ex Spent The 4th Cuddling Up With DeAndre Hopkins

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, April 30, 2024

NYDN – Colleen Crowley, Johnny Manziel’s ex-girlfriend, probably has a better chance of being on an NFL sideline this season than the former Browns quarterback.

Crowley was spotted with Houston Texans receiver DeAndre Hopkins in an Instagram picture he posted with his arm around her as the two celebrated the Fourth of July in Malibu.

The post was captioned, “H-Town in Malibu.”

Hopkins, 24, is a rising star who is coming off his best season with career-high 111 receptions, 1,521 yards and 11 touchdowns.

Ever been through a breakup? Then you know the worst part obviously. When the Instagrams and Snapchats and Facebook pictures start going up with other guys. Or maybe not even with other guys, maybe just a girls group shot at the club, but you see the guys lurking in the background and know exactly what’s about to happen 10 minutes after the picture. Mind playing all sorts of tricks on you as you imagine the most graphic things you’ve ever witnessed in a porno. Now imagine you’re a high profile celebrity in the middle of a downward spiral and those pictures are being posted on legitimate news websites with blurbs talking about the “rising star” coming off a “career season” who’s got his arm firmly draped around her.

And that ain’t no hover-hand either. That’s pretty much the exact opposite of a hover-hand. That arm is as comfortable as can be right where it is. Brutal, just brutal. And on the 4th of July no less.
