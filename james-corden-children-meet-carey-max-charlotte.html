Biography of James Corden

Corden’s success on “Gavin & Stacey” led to him being offered a number of hosting gigs, including the BRIT Awards and the Tony Awards. In 2015, he became the host of “The Late Late Show with James Corden” on CBS, which has become one of the most popular late-night talk shows in the United States.

Throughout his career, Corden has also appeared in a number of movies, including “Into the Woods,” “Peter Rabbit,” and “The Prom.” He has won several awards, including a Tony Award for his performance in the play “One Man, Two Guvnors.”
