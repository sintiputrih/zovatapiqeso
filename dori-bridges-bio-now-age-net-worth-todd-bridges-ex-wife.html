Where Is Todd Bridges Wife, Dori Bridges Now? Her Bio, Age, Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 15, 2024

The love lives of the Celebrity Big Brother contestants have always been a great matter of interest to its fans. And now that the show’s back for its season 3, its participant Todd Bridges’ history with ex-wife Dori Bridges has resurfaced over the internet.

So, we bring you this Dori Bridges Bio, telling the story from this ex-wife’s perspective. 

Meet Todd Bridges Ex-Wife, Dori Bridges

Dori Bridges married the Diff’rent Strokes actor Todd Bridges back in 1998 and the two went on to cherish 14 years of married life until 2012. According to the couple, they gradually drifted apart over the years, and their relationship turned into something that could no longer be fixed.

“It is confirmed that after 14 years of marriage, the Bridges have decided that disunion is in the best interest of their collective futures,” a representative shared.

But though the pair went their separate ways, Dori and Todd mentioned that they would be “mutually committed to the well-being of their son.”

Moreover, after the split, Todd took it to his Twitter to praise his ex-wife, “Life is full of changes. She’s a great mother…I’m glad to have had the years to know her and have a great kid with her as we know, people grow apart and we did,” he wrote.

As for their kid, the pair welcomed a son named Spencir the same year they got married.

Spencir, who’s also an actor by profession, turned 23 on July 15, 2021. He’s an Art and Design, Woodbury University graduate who’s graced movies and TV shows including The Three Gifts (2009), iCarly (2009), The Starter Wife (2008), Daddy Day Camp (2007), ER (2005), and many more.

Did you know: Spencir contracted COVID just when the world was first introduced to the deadly virus in 2020. Fortunately, the actor recovered with only minor symptoms.

Where Is Todd Bridges Ex-Wife Dori Bridges Now?

Todd Bridges’ ex-wife Dori Bridges is now (as of 2022) happily married to a man named Gene Watson. The two were married on November 24, 2018, after knowing each other for over 2 decades.

Commemorating their 3rd anniversary, Dori recently took it to her FB on Nov 2021 to write, “Happy 3rd anniversary my love!”

Overall, she seemed proud of her “sexy man.” It’s no wonder he often starred in her socials.

Furthermore, he was even Dori’s FB profile pic, the last we checked.

Trivia: Gene receives his birthday wishes on September 20.

Dori Bridges Age

Dori Bridges was born on July 7, 1975. That made her 46 years of age in 2022. She is 10 years younger than he ex-husband Todd Bridges who was born on May 27, 1965.

Corresponding to her birthday, Dori is of the Cancer zodiac.

Fun Fact: On Dori’s 2017 birthday, her husband Gene made her the same wonderful meal that he used to make for her over 25 years ago.

Dori Bridges Job

Dori Bridges is a special education assistant who worked at LAUSD, and the Cherokee County School District. Reportedly, she joined Cherokee back in Dec 2017 and was employed there as of 2022.

Besides being an educator, Dori is also an actress who’s appeared in movies and videos such as Foster Babies (2007), Big Ball’n (2007), Black Ball (2003), After Diff’rent Strokes: When the Laughter Stopped (2000), and Building Bridges (2000).

As for her education, Dori graduated from Trion High School in 1993 and went on to study English major at Jacksonville State University for a year. Later, she then joined Kennesaw State University in Feb 2017 to study psychology.
