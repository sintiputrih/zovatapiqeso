Erik von Detten Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, March 17, 2024

Who is Erik von Detten?

American Erik von Detten is a multiple Young Artist Award-nominated former actor and voice artist. Detten is renowned as the voice behind the character Sid in the movie, Toy Story. 

Next, he will appear in the series, The Tonopah Five as Erik. Currently, the movie is in post-production.

Who are the parents of Erik von Detten?

Erik von Detten was born on 3rd October 1982 in San Diego, California. His birth name is Erik Thomas von Detten. He is of British-Irish ethnicity with Germanic and Russian ancestry.

He is the son of Susan von Detten (mother) and Volker von Detten (father). His father, Volker a German immigrant and a professional photographer. He has four siblings including three sisters; Dolly, Britta, and Andrea von Detten, and a brother, Timothy von Detten.

Moving to his academics, he completed his elementary school at a local public school in El Segundo.

Erik von Detten- Professional Career

In 1991, he started his career as an actor with the movie, All I Want For Christmas as a choir. However, he came into the limelight when he made his appearance in the TV series, Days of Our Lives as Nicholas Alamain. The character, Nicholas in the series became one of the milestones of his initial career.

Following that, he featured in the TV shows like Night Sins, In the Best of FamiliesL Marriage, Pride & Madness, A Season of Hope, and A Season of Hope and Kidnapped: In the Lines of Duty in some character roles.

Later, in 1995, he featured the fantasy-adventure movie, Escape to Witch Mountain as the main character, Danny. The movie became one of the milestones in his acting career.

Apart from being an actor, he is also a successful voice artist. His journey as the voice artist started with the animated TV series, Aaahh!!! Real Monsters. In the year 1997, he voiced the character, Toy Story Treats as a Sid Phillips. However, his role in the series eas only limited to the episode, Woody’s Nightmares. 

After that, he was associated with the movies like The Wild Thornberrys as Jaguar, The Legend of Tarzan as Flynt, and Avatar: The Last Airbender as Chan.

How much does Erik von Detten earn?

As of 2023, he has an estimated net worth of $2 million. His earnings as an actor were in the range of $21k to $215k.

Besides that, as a voice actor, his salary is in the range of $29k – $586k.

Body Measurements: Height & Weight

He has blue eyes with brunette hair. He stands at a height of 6 feet and 1 inch and weighs around 70 kg.
