Roger Clemens is a famous American former professional baseball pitcher who played 24 seasons in Major League Baseball.

Table of Content

Roger Clemens Biography: Age, height, birthplace, parents, siblings, education

Roger Clemens was born on August 4, 1962, in Dayton, Ohio, in the United States of America. He is the son of Bill Clemens and Bess Clemens. His siblings are Randy Clemens, Bonnie Booher, Gary Clemens, Janet Johnson, Brenda Pavlovich, and Richard Clemens.

Roger Clemens family: Who are his wife and children?

Roger Clemens is married to Debra Lynn Godfrey. They tied the knot in 1984 and are blessed with four children Kody Clemens, Kacy Austin Clemens, Kory Allen Clemens, and Koby Clemens.

What teams did Roger Clemens play for?

Roger Clemens played for Yankees, Houston Astros, Toronto Blue Jays, and Red Sox.

Is Roger Clemens Baseball Hall of Famer?

Roger Clemens has not been elected to the Baseball Hall of Fame and is now off the ballot.

Roger Clemens net worth

Roger Clemens has an estimated net worth of $60 million. He made more money from playing baseball.

Roger Clemens Instagram

Roger Clemens is on Instagram as @rogerclemens with more than 26,000 followers.
