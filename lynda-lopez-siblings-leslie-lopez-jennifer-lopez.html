Lynda Lopez siblings: Leslie Lopez, Jennifer Lopez

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

Lynda Lopez is a famous American journalist and author based in New York City. Lydia has anchored many media platforms and authored the book AOC: The Fearless Rise and Powerful Resonance of Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez, published by St. Martin’s Press, in 2020. She is also a co-founder of Nuyorican Productions, an American production company founded in 2001 with Benny Medina which became active in 2006 with the release of South Beach.

Lynda received her primary and secondary education while growing up in the Bronx. After graduating from Preston High School in 1989, she enrolled at the C.W. Post Campus of Long Island University, where she majored in broadcasting and communications.

Lynda Lopez siblings: Leslie Lopez, Jennifer Lopez

Leslie Lopez and Jennifer Lopez are the two elder sisters of Lynda Lopez.

Jenifer is an American pop star and movie actress. Jennifer was born on Thursday, July 24, 1969, in The Bronx, New York, United States. Jennifer landed her first movie role in Selena in 1997, and same year, she starred in Anaconda and. Starring in “Out of Sight” in 1998 Jennifer established herself as the highest-paid Hispanic actress in Hollywood. Jennifer became the first Hispanic actress to earn over US$1 million for a film.

Leslie Lopez, on the other hand,  is the morning meteorologist for the news channel – ABC7 Eyewitness News. She provided weekday weather reports for the 4am, 5am, and 6am newscasts. Leslie joined ABC7 in 2016 and received her Bachelor of Science degree from Mississippi State University, where she studied broadcast meteorology.
