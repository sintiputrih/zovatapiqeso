Bryan Dunagan, Highland Park Presbyterian Church senior pastor, passes away suddenly

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, October 8, 2024

The sudden passing of Reverend Bryan Dunagan has cast a profound sense of sorrow over the community, as the news of his untimely departure continues to resonate within the hearts of those who had the privilege of working alongside him. Reverend Dunagan, who served as the senior pastor of Highland Park Presbyterian Church for nine years, was recognized for his radiant spirit, unwavering dedication, and commitment to fostering a community of faith and compassion.

Described as a beacon of light and elegance, Reverend Dunagan’s dedication to clean living, both in body and spirit, underscored his unwavering commitment to his principles and his devout practice of leading by example. His exceptional level of physical fitness served as a testament to his holistic approach to life, inspiring those around him to embrace a life of balance and spiritual fulfillment.

In the wake of his passing, an outpouring of heartfelt condolences and tributes from the community serves as a testament to the impact Reverend Dunagan had on the lives of those he shepherded. As the Dunagan family grapples with the profound loss, the community stands united in offering their support and prayers, providing solace during this period of mourning and reflection.

While the specific details surrounding Reverend Bryan Dunagan’s passing remain undisclosed, the community awaits further information with the hope that a deeper understanding of the circumstances will provide comfort and closure for those who were touched by his life and teachings. The public remains vigilant for any updates regarding the Reverend’s passing, reflecting the profound respect and admiration he garnered throughout his years of dedicated service to the church and the community.

The Highland Park Presbyterian Church community is reeling from the profound loss of their esteemed Senior Pastor, Bryan Dunagan, son of long-time TIADA Counsel Michael Dunagan, who passed away in the early hours of Thursday, October 26. As the church congregation and the entire community grapple with the heartbreaking news, the Highland Park Presbyterian Church extends their deepest condolences to the Dunagan family and all those affected by this tragic loss.

In a poignant message, the church shared the shocking news of Pastor Bryan Dunagan’s sudden passing, underscoring the profound impact of the loss on the congregation and the wider community. Encouraging prayers and unity during this time of grief, the church calls upon the congregation to lean on faith and seek solace in the collective support and guidance offered during this difficult period.

As the community prepares to honor and commemorate the life and legacy of Pastor Bryan Dunagan, the Highland Park Presbyterian Church announces the memorial service scheduled for 3 pm on Wednesday, November 1, in the Sanctuary. In an effort to accommodate the expected attendance, the church offers parking assistance for the mobility impaired on Shannon Lane, while also providing online viewing options for those unable to attend in person.
