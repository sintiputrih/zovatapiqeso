Sanjana Ganesan Wiki/Biography

Career

Sanjana Ganesan started her career as an IT professional and a part-time model. She has been featured in the Femina Style Diva fashion show and ended up as a finalist in the year 2012. The next year, she has participated in the Femina Miss India Pune competition and again ended up as a finalist.

She has also the competition named Femina Officially Gorgeous competition. After that, she has been featured in the popular TV reality show MTV Splitsvilla Season 7 but she needs to quit the show as she got injured during the show.

In the year 2016, she joined the Star Sports channel as an anchor and sports presenter. She rose to fame after she hosted the talk show named Match-Point during the Indian Premier League and was also hired by Kolkata Knight Riders to host a how for their team regularly. She interviewed many popular players and Shah Rukh Khan during this show.

She has also hosted some other popular sports shows like Indian Super League, Badminton Super League, and IPL Auction.

She is always against the use of tobacco and even participated in a campaign in the year 2018 which mainly focuses on the use of tobacco.

She also hosted the Cricket World Cup 2019 from the Indian side and hosted the show titled Dil Se India.
