How Did Melyssa Davies Meet James Murray?

The couple first encountered one another in 2018 at the premiere of James’ novel, Awakened. Despite the age difference, they quickly became friends and then began dating.

Their great age gap of 19 years is what has drawn the most attention; Melyssa Murray is 28, and James is 47. Together they share a pet dog, Penelope, fondly called Penny.
