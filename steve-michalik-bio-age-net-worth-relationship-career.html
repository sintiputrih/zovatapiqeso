How did Steve Michalik start his Professional Career?

Steve, a Brooklyn, New York, resident, participated in the 1968 Teenage Mr. America – AAU and finished third. He was later named Mr. Western New York State in 1969.

Steve participated in several bodybuilding contests in 1971, including Mr. Apollo and Mr. USA. In addition, he earned the moniker “Most Muscular” after winning Mr. America in 1972. Along with other competitions, he took part in the Grand Prix, Night of Champions, and World Pro Championships.
