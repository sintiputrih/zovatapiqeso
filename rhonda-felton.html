Rhonda Felton is an American singer known on TV for her auditioning in American Idol hosted in Milwaukee. On that audition, she sang “One Night Only” from the musical “Dreamgirls,” her performance brought judge and music legend, Lionel Richie to tears.

She cried as Katy Perry and Luke Bryan praised her and gave her a golden ticket to Hollywood on the set of American Idol.
