Barbara Walters Net Worth Explored, Heres How She Amassed Her Millions

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, April 6, 2024

Barbara Walters is a renowned television personality and broadcast journalist with over 170 million dollars in net worth.

She has established her place in the fan’s hearts due to her engaging interviewing skills and hosted several television shows, including Today, The View, 20/20, and the ABC Evening News.

The television show host worked as a journalist from 1951 until her retirement in 2015. She initiated her career in journalism as a segment producer and writer for women’s interest topics on The Today Show in the early 1960s.

Because of the reporter’s success with viewers, Walters began appearing more frequently on the show. Then, in 1974, Barbara got promoted to co-host, making history as the first woman to occupy such a position on an American news program.

She continued to pave the way for women in broadcasting in 1976 when she joined Harry Reasoner as the first female co-anchor of a network evening news show on ABC Evening News. Barbara also gained fame for Barbara Walters’ 10 Most Fascinating People, an annual ABC broadcast.

She spoke with every sitting president and first lady of the United States, from Richard Nixon to Barack Obama.
