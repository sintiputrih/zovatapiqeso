Brendan Gleesons Bio, Age, Career, Net Worth, Movies, Wife

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, May 6, 2024

Brendan Gleeson is an accomplished Irish actor, director, and former teacher who has won numerous awards for his acting skills. He is well known for playing Professor Alastor Moody (Mad Eye) in three “Harry Potter” films and for playing Winston Churchill in the 2009 film “Into the Storm,” for which he won an Emmy Award. Brendan Gleeson was born to his parents Pat and Frank Gleeson on March 29, 1955 in Dublin, Ireland. He has a younger brother called Frank Gleeson. He was reportedly an avid reader of Irish playwrights like Samuel Beckett as a young child.

The actor participated in high school plays like “Waiting for Godot” and drama classes while still a teen. He also devoted a few years to the “Dublin Shakespeare Festival.”

Brendan Gleeson Career

Brendan Gleeson’s acting career began in 1990 when he landed a role in the TV show, “Dear Sarah.” He then went on to act in several other TV shows, including “The Snapper,” “Bringing It All Back Home,” and “The Treaty.” He also acted in several movies, such as “Far and Away,” “The General,” and “The Butcher Boy.” His breakout role came in 2000 when he starred in the movie “Gangs of New York,” directed by Martin Scorsese.

One of his most iconic performances came in the 2005 movie “Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire,” where he played the role of Alastor “Mad-Eye” Moody. His portrayal of the character was widely praised by critics and audiences alike, and he went on to reprise the role in the subsequent movies in the series.

The actor has also acted in several other popular movies, such as “In Bruges,” “The Guard,” and “Calvary.” His performance in “In Bruges” earned him a Golden Globe nomination for Best Actor in a Motion Picture – Comedy or Musical. He has also acted in several TV shows, such as “The Last Furlong,” “Into the Storm,” and “Mr. Mercedes.”

Apart from acting, he is also a musician. He plays the guitar and has performed in several bands, including The Brothers and The Grimleys. He has also composed music for several TV shows and movies.

The actor has been recognized for his acting talent with several awards and nominations. He has won three Irish Film and Television Awards, a British Independent Film Award, and a Primetime Emmy Award for his role in the TV movie “Into the Storm.” He has also been nominated for several other awards, such as the Academy Award, the BAFTA Award, and the Screen Actors Guild Award.

Brendan Gleeson Wife

Brendan Gleeson married his wife, Mary Weldon in 1982. The couple has four children together: Fergus, Bríona, Rúairí, and Domhnall, who is also an actor.
